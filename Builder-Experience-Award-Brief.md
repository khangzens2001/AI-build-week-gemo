# Builder Experience Award

**A pre-event warm-up track for Agentic AI Build Week (AABW).**
Form a team and build an AI tool that makes the builder experience **better during the event itself** — and the best builds get **deployed live during the week** for thousands of builders to use.

---

## The challenge

Agentic AI Build Week is five days of building across multiple venues, workshops, teammates, perks, deadlines, and judging. It's a lot to navigate — and the bigger it gets, the easier it is for a builder to miss a workshop, lose track of a deadline, or struggle to find the right team.

> **Build an AI-powered tool, agent, workflow, or experience that improves the journey for builders — above all, during Agentic AI Build Week itself.**

Your solution should help builders **act faster, cut confusion, and get more out of the week** — in the room, in real time. (Tools that help builders prepare *before* the week are welcome too, but the heart of this challenge is making the live event better.)

---

## Your week at Agentic AI Build Week (Jul 8–12, Ho Chi Minh City)

Five days, one goal: builders shipping real agents. This is the rhythm your tool should make smoother:

- **Day 1 — Enable (Jul 8):** Registration, welcome, and the official kickoff. Partner-led workshops begin across the city, and the buildathon starts.
- **Day 2 — Integrate (Jul 9):** Hands-on workshops from leading cloud, model, and AI partners. Teams start integrating the stacks they'll build on.
- **Day 3 — Design (Jul 10):** More partner workshops across venues, plus an evening community night.
- **Day 4 — Build (Jul 11):** Everyone on-site at the main venue — heads-down hackathon, an evening AI night, and late-night building.
- **Day 5 — Demo (Jul 12):** Demo Day — teams present, judges score, and awards close out the week.

**Days 1–3** run as partner workshops across the city; **Days 4–5** bring everyone together on-site for the hackathon and Demo Day. Throughout, builders juggle venues, workshops, teammates, deadlines, perks, and judging.

**That mix is exactly what your Builder Experience Award tool should improve** — helping builders find the right workshop, get to the right venue, meet teammates, track deadlines, claim perks, get support, and stay in the loop while they build.

## What you can build

Think about a builder's day at the event — where do they get stuck? A few directions:

- Team matching or co-founder matching tools
- Personalized event copilots ("what's on now, where, and what should I do next?")
- Workshop recommenders and schedule navigators
- Venue / map / "where do I go" helpers
- Resource and perk discovery tools
- Discord or WhatsApp support bots
- Submission deadline trackers and reminders
- Mentor matching systems
- Builder onboarding flows
- Community tools for builders to showcase progress during the week

These are **just references** — the ideas are yours. **Surprise us, and make a builder's week easier.**

**Keep it self-contained.** Build with your own, public, or mock data — you don't need access to any internal systems. If you win, we handle the integration to deploy it. Just include a short README so it's easy to run.

---

## What is NOT allowed

To keep submissions genuinely useful, these will be **disqualified**:

- Plain AI chatbots / thin LLM wrappers — a chat box over an LLM with no real workflow or product around it
- A single static prompt or "prompt template" submitted as a project
- Generic tools unrelated to the builder or event experience
- Clones, repackaged, or pre-existing projects submitted as new work
- Slides, mockups, or concepts with no working demo / prototype
- Anything that just relabels an existing chatbot UI without solving a real builder problem

---

## What makes a strong submission

- Solves a real builder pain point — ideally one builders hit *during* the event
- Usable by Agentic AI Build Week participants, in real time
- Shows a working demo or prototype
- Uses AI meaningfully, not as a gimmick
- Simple enough for builders to understand and use quickly
- Has potential to go live during Agentic AI Build Week

## How it works

1. **Submit on Devpost** within the track, before the deadline.
2. **Round 1 — community voting** (on Discord) narrows the field to a **top 20** shortlist.
3. The **top 20 is revealed on June 23** at the kickoff webinar and **featured on our landing page**.
4. **Round 2 — public voting** on the top 20 runs on the landing page **until June 29 (11:59 PM)**. The most-voted build wins **People's Choice** — so rally your community to vote and share.
5. **Winners are announced on July 1.**

A public vote keeps it fun — but quality still counts, so a popular-but-weak build can't win on votes alone.

## Your journey, step by step

How you move through the **track** (the warm-up contest):

1. **Discover** — hear about the track via Discord, Luma, socials, ambassadors, or the webinar.
2. **Register on Luma** — sign up for AABW on the event page (if you haven't already).
3. **Join Discord & verify** — unlock the track channels and the #wishlist of community ideas.
4. **Join the track on Devpost** — register, then create or join a team for the Builder Experience track.
5. **Read the brief** — review the challenge and what's allowed; grab an idea from #wishlist or bring your own.
6. **Build (~1 week)** — build a self-contained tool with your own/mock data. Share progress in Discord.
7. **Submit on Devpost** — post your project: a short description, how it helps builders during the event, a demo (video or live link), and your repo.
8. **Get your reward** — every team that submits gets a sticker + a Discord track label.
9. **Round 1 voting** — the community votes on Discord to narrow to a top 20 shortlist.
10. **Top 20 revealed** — live at the June 23 kickoff webinar, then featured on our landing page.
11. **Round 2 voting** — vote for the top 20 on the landing page until June 29; **winners announced July 1**.
12. **Deployment** — winning tools go live **during AABW (Jul 8–12)**, serving thousands of builders in the room — with the door open to keep building it with us afterward.
